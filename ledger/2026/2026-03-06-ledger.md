## Data Governance Statement

DGCP follows an **append-only data governance model**.

Records are not retroactively modified.
When clarification or correction is required,
a new record is appended to the dataset.

This preserves:

- historical traceability
- operational transparency
- evidence continuity

The dataset therefore reflects the
actual observation history of the system.

---

## Operational Principle

Truth is preserved through structure.

Observation is recorded.
Errors are not erased.
Clarifications are appended.

The system grows through evidence,
not through revision.
